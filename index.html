About SAGE Production Services

SAGE Production Services is a workspace in Montgomery County PA that employs and provides supports for survivors of commercial sexual exploitation while they transition into normal work. Our staff of survivor advocates come from a variety of backgrounds, such as manufacturing and behavioral health. We are devoted to providing the best services possible for our clientele because we know this is the best way to serve the organization. SAGE is a 501C3 nonprofit registered with the IRS.
